About Me

I am a person who is positive about every aspect of life. There are many things I like to do, to see, and to experience. I like to read, I like to write; I like to think, I like to dream; I like to talk, I like to listen. I like to look at the clouds in the sky with a blank mind, I like to do thought experiment when I cannot sleep in the middle of the night.

Education

I am a sophomore pursuing my B.Tech in the department of Chemical Enginnering, along with a minor in Computer Science, at IIT Bombay.

I completed my education till Class 10 from Modern School, Nagpur and my Intermediate from Disha Delphi Public School, Kota.

My Hobbies

I’m an incessant reader: I more or less always have a book at hand—sometimes more than one. My favorite genre is mysteries, particularly British and Scandinavian novels: I love a twisty plot and tend to pit my wits against the fictional sleuth to see whether I can figure out the solution before she/he does. I particularly admire Henning Mankell: I’ve learned more about history and politics from his mysteries than I ever was taught in school.What I truly treasure about my hobby is its power to transport me to different locations whilst I’m still on my couch. It is a low budget travel option that I often take!

My Ambitions

I have always been passionate about Engineering but given the vast array of opportunities that are available to an engineer today, I am still exploring and am yet to decide what I really want to be. But I have been always clear about one thing- and that is to become a better version of myself with every passing day.
